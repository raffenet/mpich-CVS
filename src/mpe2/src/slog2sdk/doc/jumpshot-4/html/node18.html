3.3.1.4.2 Duration Info Box

Figure: Duration Info Box shows the duration, starttime, and endtime of a time region marked by a pair of green lines.

Duration Info Box is created by right dragging a region of empty space in the timeline canvas or the time ruler canvas. The dragged region will be marked by a pair of green lines and is lightly shaded as well. One of the usages of Duration Info Box is to compare different durations to see if they are the same. For instance in Figure , the 2 Duration Info Boxes mark 2 non-overlap regions on the 3rd timeline to check if the total duration taken up by the all consecutive small states within the 2 regions are the same. As shown in the Duration Info Box, it appears the total durations are the same.
